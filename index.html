Dice Game

This is a two player dice game made using vanilla JavaScript.
The game is taken in turns and each player has a 'round score', and 'total score'.
Each turn, a player's round score accumulates per dice role. However, if they roll a 1, their turn is over and they lose their round score. After each roll, they have the option to 'Hold' their round score. This adds their 'round score' to their 'total score'. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • JavaScript / ES6
  • HTML
  • CSS
